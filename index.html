The minimal software engineering AI agent

In use by

In 2024, we built SWE-bench and SWE-agent at Princeton University and helped kickstart the coding agent revolution. These works have influenced most modern AI agents, including Claude Code, GitHub copilot and many others.

Our latest work is the mini agent (aka mini-swe-agent): It turns out that modern language models allow you to build much, much simpler agents, than anyone believed. In fact, some 100 lines of python are enough!

The mini agent is for

Developers who like to own, understand, and modify their tools
Researchers who want to benchmark, fine-tune or RL without assumptions, bloat, or surprises
Engineers who want something trivial to sandbox & to deploy anywhere
